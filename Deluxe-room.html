Deluxe AC Room Details

Deluxe AC Room

The Deluxe AC Rooms at Bhagirath Palace & Restaurant offer a perfect balance of comfort and affordability. These rooms are thoughtfully designed with a room size of 15x12 square feet, providing ample space for up to two guests. The rooms feature a double bed with cozy furnishings, ensuring a restful sleep. The interiors are stylishly decorated, reflecting a mix of modern aesthetics and traditional Rajasthani charm. Guests can enjoy the serene desert and highway views, creating a relaxing ambiance.
Each Deluxe AC Room includes essential amenities like two almirahs, a dressing table, and a 48-inch TV, making it ideal for both short and long stays. The attached bathrooms are equipped with modern fixtures, including a geyser for hot water. To ensure convenience, we provide RO-filtered water, and the rooms are equipped with refrigerators for added comfort.
