Heritage AC Room

The Heritage AC Rooms at Bhagirath Palace & Restaurant are designed to provide a luxurious and comfortable stay for our guests. With a spacious room size of 15x20 square feet, these rooms can accommodate up to three persons. The interiors are adorned with a double king-size bed, two almirahs, a dressing table, and a dining table with four chairs, offering a blend of style and practicality. Guests can relax on the three plush sofas paired with a cup table, while enjoying a scenic highway view from their room. The rooms are equipped with modern amenities such as a 48-inch TV, an attached bathroom with a geyser, a refrigerator, and RO-filtered water for added convenience.
Situated on the main four-lane Jodhpur-Jaisalmer highway, the hotel offers excellent connectivity and transportation options, making it a perfect stop for travelers. Additionally, Bhagirath Palace provides transportation services to ensure guests can explore the beautiful city of Jaisalmer without hassle. With ample parking facilities, 24-hour security, and a host of premium amenities, the Heritage AC Rooms promise a royal experience with unparalleled comfort and convenience.
