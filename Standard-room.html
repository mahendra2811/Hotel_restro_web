Standard AC Room

Our Standard AC Room offers a cozy, budget-friendly stay without compromising on comfort or heritage charm. Ideal for solo travelers or those seeking simplicity, this room is thoughtfully designed with a size of 8x10 feet, providing a compact yet comfortable space. It features a single bed, attached bathroom with geyser, RO-filtered water, and essential amenities for a convenient stay. While it doesn’t include a sofa, dining table, or dressing table, it retains the elegant heritage-inspired environment that defines our hotel. Enjoy a peaceful stay with modern conveniences, making it the perfect choice for travelers exploring Jaisalmer on a budget.
