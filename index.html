About Bhagirath Palace & Restaurant

Experience the Timeless Heritage of Jaisalmer

Nestled in the golden sands of Rajasthan, Bhagirath Palace & Restaurant is a tranquil oasis where heritage meets modern comfort. Owned by Jeevraj Singh and managed by Datar Singh, our hotel invites you to immerse yourself in the culture and traditions of Jaisalmer. From our warm hospitality to the architectural charm of our palace, every detail is designed to make your stay unforgettable.
